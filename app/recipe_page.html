Cauiflower Pizza Crust

  
recipe
by Bugra Uluyurt
South African Cuisine


Preheat the oven to 180°C/350°F/gas 4. Peel and finely slice the garlic and put it into a medium pan on a medium heat with the butter. When the butter has melted, stir in the flour.
